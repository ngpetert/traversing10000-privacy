Privacy Policy

Effective: 27 April 2026 Last Updated: 27 April 2026 Jurisdiction: Singapore (PDPA)
01

Introduction

Traversing10000 ("we", "us", "our") operates a suite of digital products including websites (including but not limited to traversing10000.com and property.traversing10000.com), mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use any of our Services.

This policy is written in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore, and takes into account international best practices including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable to users in those jurisdictions.

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services.

02

Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We do not require account registration or login for any of our current Services. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us via email or a contact form.

If you contact us directly (e.g. via email at privacy@traversing10000.com), we may retain the content of your message and your email address solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our websites or use our mobile applications, certain technical information is collected automatically by our infrastructure and third-party service providers:

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect: payment card information or financial data; government-issued identification numbers; biometric data; precise real-time location data; health or medical information; or the content of private communications.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

⚙️
Service Operation. To deliver, maintain, and improve our Services, including ensuring pages load correctly and features function as intended.
📊
Analytics and Performance. To understand how users interact with our Services in aggregate — for example, which pages are most visited, how long users stay, and which features are used most frequently. This helps us improve the quality and relevance of our content.
🔒
Security and Fraud Prevention. To detect, investigate, and prevent malicious activity, abuse, or security incidents on our infrastructure.
⚖️
Legal Compliance. To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests in Singapore and other applicable jurisdictions.
🤖
AI-Assisted Analysis. Some of our Services use artificial intelligence tools (including Manus AI) to process publicly available data (such as URA property transaction records) to generate analytical insights. This processing does not involve your personal data — it operates entirely on public datasets.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

05

Mobile Application

If you use our mobile application(s), the following additional practices apply:

📱
Permissions. Our mobile app requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality. We will always explain why a permission is needed before requesting it. We do not request access to your contacts, microphone, camera, or location unless a specific feature explicitly requires it.
💾
Data Storage. Any data you generate or save within the app (such as saved searches or preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or share it.
🔔
Push Notifications. If you opt in to push notifications, your device token is used solely to deliver notifications you have requested. You may withdraw consent at any time through your device settings.
🏪
App Store Platforms. If you download our app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may collect data about your download and usage in accordance with their own privacy policies. We have no control over this data collection.
👶
Children. Our mobile applications are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@traversing10000.com.
06

Data Retention

We retain automatically collected analytics data (in aggregated, anonymised form) for up to 24 months, after which it is deleted or further anonymised.

If you contact us by email, we retain your correspondence for up to 12 months after the matter is resolved, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

We do not retain personal data beyond what is necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

07

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect information processed through our Services, including:

08

International Data Transfers

Our Services are operated from Singapore. If you access our Services from outside Singapore, your information may be transferred to and processed in Singapore or other countries where our third-party service providers operate (including the United States, where Cloudflare and Google maintain data centres).

We ensure that any such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
